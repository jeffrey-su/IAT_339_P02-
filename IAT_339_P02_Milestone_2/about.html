About Us

Luna is an online retailer dedicated to offering snow skin mooncakes with unique flavours and healthier ingredients. Snow skin mooncakes (冰皮月餅) are a non-baked Chinese dessert traditionally enjoyed cold during the Mid-Autumn Festival and originating from Hong Kong. With a growing interest in Asian desserts in Vancouver.

Our Mission

We aim to introduce this traditional Chinese delicacy to the public, particularly the younger generation. We prioritize customer health through our ingredient choices, opting for fruits and glutinous rice as a healthier alternative to traditional mooncakes. Additionally, specializing in flavours, LUNA offers a diverse range of innovative flavours, catering to food enthusiasts who appreciate exploring new experiences.
