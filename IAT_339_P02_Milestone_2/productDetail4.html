INGREDIENTS

HOW TO PRESERVE?

Our Snowskin Mooncakes are delivered in frozen condition and should be kept in the freezer immediately. It will take about 15 mins to thaw and once thawed, it is not advisable to freeze again. If not thawed, they can be kept in the freezer for up to 6 months.
