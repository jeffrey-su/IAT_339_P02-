Branding Basics

What does it offer?

[PLACEHOLDER] is an online retailer dedicated to offering snow skin mooncakes with unique flavours and healthier ingredients. Snow skin mooncakes (冰皮月餅) are a non-baked Chinese dessert traditionally enjoyed cold during the Mid-Autumn Festival and originating from Hong Kong. With a growing interest in Asian desserts in Vancouver, we aim to introduce this traditional Chinese delicacy to the public, particularly the younger generation. We prioritize customer health through our ingredient choices, opting for fruits and glutinous rice as a healthier alternative to traditional mooncakes. Additionally, specializing in flavours, [PLACEHOLDER] offers a diverse range of modern and innovative flavours, catering to food enthusiasts who appreciate exploring new culinary experiences.

Who is [PLACEHOLDER] to potential customers? Why might someone to purchase a product from us?

[PLACEHOLDER]'s target audience for these products includes food enthusiasts, health-conscious individuals, and families. With our diverse range of unique flavours, we cater to food enthusiasts who enjoy exploring new desserts and flavours. Additionally, snow skin mooncakes feature healthier ingredients compared to traditional mooncakes, making them appealing to those seeking a healthier alternative. Moreover, as mooncakes symbolize "completeness and reunion" in Chinese culture, families can enjoy them together. Our unique flavours also attract the younger generation, allowing them to experience and learn more about the culture through our products.

For the typefaces, Neue Montreal Mono from Pangram Pangram suited the clean and minimal aesthetic well at h1 and h2. Mono space typefaces are easy to read and fit the "Form and Function" styling. as for body copy, Pangran Pangram's Supply Sans is blocky and industrial which pairs well with the clean bold lines of Neue Montreal Mono.

Going into usability, System fonts should be used if the above mentioned typefaces are not available or cannot be injected in the markup. Choosing from the list of system typefaces available, Impact for h1, Tahoma for h2 and Courier New for body copy are the most similar to the typefaecs above. Though not a perfect match, they still have similar qualities and funtion the same roles.

Thoughtfully designed products are what make the company unique. To fit that, a palatte that allows for the products to speak for themselves is used. A white background helps to contrast with dark environments the customer may be in allowing for higher visibility and contrast. It also allows for the product to stand out against the stark white. Dividers are in a grey, not black as not to have too drastic of a change and ease the customer into the interface, rather than being uncomfortable. Finally, the bright blue accent allows for colour to be introduced as to provide more contrast between both the products, white space, and the dividing colour. The blue is similar to the blue used on blueprints harkoning back to the industrial style of the company.
